About Us

Aman Airline is the flag carrier airline of India, headquartered at New Delhi. It is owned by "Sethi & Sharma Limited", a government-owned enterprise, and operates a fleet of Airbus and Boeing aircraft serving 102 domestic and international destinations. The airline has its hub at Indira Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi, alongside several focus cities across India. Aman Airline is the largest international carrier out of India with an 18.6% market share. Over 60 international destinations are served by Aman Airline across four continents. The airline became the 27th member of Star Alliance on 11 July 2014
